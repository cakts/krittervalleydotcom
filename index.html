what is this game?

KRITTER VALLEY is a mind-bending open-world survival platformer that follows a lonely human after the events of SUNDEATH, a failed attempt to harvest energy from the sun that mutates the majority of all living creatures into hostile KRITTERS. A memoryless human receives interdimensional powers from their mutation, allowing them to explore the world on the sides of a cube.

Survive the apocalyptic KRITTER VALLEY by collecting supplies, escaping KRITTERS, and uncovering the mystery of SUNDEATH. Bend space to your will and use your many movement options to travel through the open-world environment with style.
